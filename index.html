One-time setup
  • Show your bookmarks bar: Ctrl+Shift+B
  • Drag the blue button above to the bar
  • Done — you only do this once
Every conversation
  • Open your Copilot share URL and wait for it to load
  • Click blkboxreader in your bookmarks bar
  • copilot-chat-[date].md downloads instantly ✓
Why a bookmarklet and not a URL field? Copilot share pages are rendered entirely by JavaScript — no external tool can fetch their content from a URL. The bookmarklet runs inside your browser tab where the page is already live, extracts the conversation text, formats it as Markdown, and downloads the file. No server, no API key, no intermediate steps.
Upload a Ctrl+S saved HTML file Open the Copilot share page, press Ctrl+S, save as Webpage, Complete, then upload the file below. Requires a Gemini API key.

How to get the HTML source
  1. Open your Copilot share link and wait for it to fully load
  2. Press Ctrl+U to open View Source in a new tab
  3. Press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy all
  4. Paste below and click Convert

How to get the plain text
  1. Open your Copilot share link and wait for it to fully load
  2. Press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy all visible text
  3. Paste below and click Convert
